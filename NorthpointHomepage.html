A Word from Pastor Al

Hi! As Pastor of NorthPoint Community Church I welcome you to not only this website but also to our worship services in Hartland, Wisconsin.


We offer two styles of worship and our messages are powerfully relevant…and with no doubt the people are great!


We welcome you to our new worship facility which I call “The little church on the hill!” I said “new” but the church was built over 100 years ago. We are located at 400 W. Capitol Drive in Hartland. The contemporary worship is at 9:00 AM and the traditional worship is at 10:30 AM with organist Jon Rinka. Sunday School is during the 9:00 AM worship! The children go to Sunday School following the opening of the worship service. Click on “sermons” to hear the most recent messages!

Praise Bands

One of the great blessings of our congregation is the music. We have been blessed with many great musicians and vocalists which makes our worship such a great experience. And the really wonderful thing is the fact that they are all volunteers who want to use their abilities to the glory of God. Join us for worship and see for yourself!
